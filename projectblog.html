Rasberry Pi NAS

May 2023

My First Project

For an entire year, I watched approved RaspberryPi resellers hoping to find a kit in stock and go through with the project. This past May I was able to go through with the project after purchasing the CanaKit 4GB Extreme Kit. The kit included the board, case, an SD card reader, SD card preloaded with NOOBS(New Out Of Box Software), and 2 Micro HDMi cords. After a brief and simple assembly I plugged it into my monitor and started configuration.
